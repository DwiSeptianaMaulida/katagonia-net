    About Us

    KATA

    means “word” in Indonesian. As an agency which focuses on translation and interpreting services, we believe the industry we serve here requires us more than rendering one message from one language to another language. It is translation and MORE. It’s about communications, intentions and expected outcome from the exchange of messages that we help translate/interpret for our clients. The people we serve need translation/interpreting services, but what they actually need are mutual understanding with their counterparts, deals being made with their business partners, work delivered to their clients, simply beyond the translation output. Thus, it is imperative that we understand more our about clients, their mission, their style of communications, the jargons and terms they use, and perhaps other adjacent services that our team at Katagonia can help provide. This kind of mindset in serving our client is at the heart of Katagonia.

    GO

    signifies a sense of mobility and urgency. It is even more relevant to the ongoing challenges we face on a daily basis as a result of the Covid-19 pandemic. The word “go” activates that alarm in our team to move forward regardless the limiting factors, to adapt to the constantly evolving demands and requirements from the clients, and more importantly to appreciate each step and process involved throughout the journey. The word “go” more importantly is a constant reminder of the importance to simply get the job done. The pandemic-related challenges have put significant amount of limitations and pressures on how we conduct our business, but our team would focus on the best probable ways/alternatives to still deliver and keep our promise to our client.

    NIA

    means “bright” (of Welsh origin) . We are both proud and humbled by the skills and experiences that our translators and interpreters bring to the table. Most of our translators and interpreters have been in the industry for more than eight years. Their mastery of the translation/interpreting skills and the experiences they accumulate over the years are the bright light that makes Katagonia a reliable and dependable LSP. The majority of them have worked in the tri-sector world (private sector, public sector and social sector), enabling them to adapt swiftly between seemingly unrelated topics of assignment.

    Our Services

    We provide quality English to Bahasa Indonesia (vice versa) translation / interpreting services with an emphasis on high level of accuracy translation/interpreting whilst at the same time prioritizing our client’s deadline for delivery.

    Translation

    We translate business text intended for clients as well as material intended for internal company communication. Sworn or regular translation. Good translation means good communication, resulting in growth due to better relationship with your clients and within the company.

    Interpreting

    We have professional bahasa Indonesia – English language speaker able to do simultaneous interpreting, consecutive interpreting or any ad-hoc interpreting to tailor your needs, whether a small group discussion or a large seminar we will help you to deliver the message
